PASSIVE INCOME

Passive income by sharing internet

  • Rishikesh Prasad
  • Dec 5, 2022
  • 14 min read

The Internet has become an inseparable part of our lives that powers virtually every aspect of the modern economy, well what if you can transform your Internet connection into a passive income stream by merely sharing your Internet connection, Intrigued?

Here’s a list of applications sorted by their earning potential.

Mysterium Node

Mysterium Network is a decentralized network of computers that provides peer-to-peer proxy services. It allows the Node runners to share unused internet bandwidth and earn through the MYST crypto while simultaneously contributing the mysterium network to grow.

Platform: Android, iOS, Windows, Linux, MacOS.
Payout: Crypto (Myst).
Rate: Varies depending on the services being utilised by the client.
Requisite: Internet connection (prefer ethernet).

Setup: To start earning set up MystNodes on Windows, Linux, macOS, Android or iOS.

  1. Start by signing up on MystNodes and confirming your email.

  2. On the MystNodes onboarding page, select your platform and continue with node installation.

  3. After installation open your NodeUi http://localhost:4449.

  4. Complete the Quick Onboarding process by entering your Myst token’s wallet address (from Metamask or any other crypto wallet).

  5. Node setup is now complete, and its stats can be viewed on the MystNodes dashboard.

# Pro tip

To maximize your chances of earning:

  • Prefer ethernet over WiFi.
  • Try enabling additional services on your NodeUI Dashboard.
  • Uptime matters; the moment you are disconnected from a client, they will be reconnected to a more competitive node.
  • Your internet speed & Geographical location matter.
  • Do not use VPNs based on higher earning countries; your node would display as a data center node and wouldn't be able to get past geo-restrictions.

Earnapp

Earnapp allows users to earn money by sharing their internet bandwidth with businesses in need of network resources. They get the same web content that any normal user will see when visiting a webpage in anonymous mode. For example:

  • Average review score of a product on an e-commerce website

  • The price of a London to New York flight ticket on an airline site

  • The text of an ad on a social network

  • Search results of a specific query (e.g. “best pizza in town”)

Your device works as a proxy service enabling clients to route traffic through your device.

Platform: Android, iOS, Windows, Linux, MacOS.
Payout: Paypal
Rate: $1/10GB
Min payout: $2.5 (auto payment supported)
Requisite: Internet connection.

Setup: Begin by registering at Earnapp and installing the Earnapp app for your device.

PacketStream

PacketStream is another proxy service app that lets you earn by allowing clients to route internet traffic anonymously through your device and earn based on the amount of data transferred.

Platform: Windows, Linux, MacOS.
Payout: Paypal
Rate: $1/10GB
Min payout: $5
Requisite: Internet connection.

Setup: Simply register yourself at PacketStream and install the app for your device from the “Download” section.

Honeygain

Honeygain has been a long-standing platform that enables users to monetize their unused internet bandwidth. By allowing the platform to utilize excess bandwidth, users contribute to tasks like content delivery and market research, facilitating peer-to-peer (P2P) traffic through their devices. You also get the initial sign-up bonus of $3, coupon-code RISHI0A188.

Platform: Android, Windows, Linux, MacOS.
Payout: Paypal | Crypto (Jmpt token)
Rate: $1/10GB
Min payout: $20 (Paypal) | Crypto (no min limit)
Requisite: Internet connection.

Setup: Register an account at Honeygain and install the app for your device.
